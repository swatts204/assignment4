SNEAKER GENI(US)

Your Average Sneaker Head

I became infatuated with sneakers at the tender age of 10. My parents had always bought me nice sneakers. However, it wasn't until my 10th birthday, when I fell in love with the Air Jordan XV. Ever since that day I walked to the sneaker store by myself and bought these sneakers with my own money I had collected for my birthday, I knew that sneakers would have a permanent place in my life.
